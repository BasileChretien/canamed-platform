# Code of Conduct

This project follows the **Contributor Covenant, version 2.1**.

Read the full text here:
https://www.contributor-covenant.org/version/2/1/code_of_conduct/

## In our own words

We want this project to be a welcoming space for medical students,
educators, researchers and developers from any background, country, level
of experience or language. Be kind, assume good faith, give credit
generously, and disagree with ideas rather than people.

## Scope

The Covenant applies to all project spaces, including this repository
(issues, pull requests, discussions, commit messages), the live platform
when used by enrolled participants, project mailing lists, and any
in-person or online events organised under the CaNaMED name.

## Reporting

If you experience or witness behaviour that violates the Covenant, please
report it to **`canamed-ethics@unicaen.fr`**. Reports are read by the
project leads (the named PIs at Universite de Caen Normandie and Nagoya
University) and treated confidentially. Anonymous reports are accepted
but harder to act on.

We will respond within 5 working days. For urgent safety concerns at a
live workshop, contact your local facilitator first.

## Enforcement

The project leads follow the Covenant's enforcement ladder (correction,
warning, temporary ban, permanent ban) as appropriate to the severity
and pattern of the behaviour. Decisions are documented and the reporter
is informed of the outcome.
